Privacy Policy — PERFECT PITCH Piano Tuner

Effective date: January 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PERFECT PITCH Company handles information when you use the PERFECT PITCH Piano Tuner PRO and LITE mobile applications (the “App”).

1. Information We Collect and Use

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not directly collect, store, or see any personal information such as your name, email address, or location.

2. Microphone Access and Audio Processing

The App requires access to your device's microphone to detect the pitch of your piano strings.

  • On-Device Processing: All audio analysis is performed strictly on your device in real-time.
  • No Recording or Transmission: We do not record, save, or transmit your audio to any servers.
  • External Devices: The App supports routing audio through Bluetooth speakers, headphones, or earbuds for your convenience, but the processing remains local to your device.

3. Advertising (LITE Version)

The LITE version of the App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. To show these ads, AdMob may collect and use certain data, including:

  • Device Identifiers: Such as your Android Advertising ID.
  • Device Information: Your device model, operating system version, and IP address (used to determine approximate location for localized ads).
  • Ad Interactions: Information about which ads you see and whether you click on them.

This data is collected directly by Google. You can manage your ad preferences or opt-out of personalized advertising through your Android device settings (Settings > Google > Ads).

The PRO version of the App has no ads.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. The only data "shared" is the technical information automatically collected by Google AdMob to serve and measure the performance of advertisements within the App.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is a utility for musicians and is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by an updated "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
